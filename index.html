About Me

Yazhou Zhu (Member, IEEE) is currently a Systems Research Engineer/Scientist at Intel Corporation. Since 2008, he has worked as a research development engineer, with a focus on signal processing and AI algorithms for wireless radar sensing and wireless communication, including:

  • Wireless radar sensing/positioning signal processing
  • Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC)
  • Wireless/satellite (NTN) communication (algorithm/protocol)
  • Wireless pre-standardization
  • Signal optimization algorithm
  • Applied AI/ML
  • Software development with Python/TensorFlow/C/C++/matlab

Research Interests

  • 5G/6G and Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN)
  • Joint sensing & communication
  • 3GPP standards for NTN
  • Resource optimization
  • Massive MIMO-OFDM transceiver prototyping
